Atoms and Molecules
-------------------

 - Atoms and molecules: Use standard chemical notation. Eg: "H20". Isotopes are denoted by a pound symbol "#" followed by the isotopic number followed by the chemical symbol. Eg: "#3He" for Helium-3.
